PWP milestone 1

Purpose: This project will be a campaign site to convey to the user why the candidate's beliefs and solutions to the challenges of the state make them the best option for state senate.

Audience: This site is designed for people who are curious about the candidate who is running . The target audience would be the average constituant in senate district 21 who is concerned about the numerous problems plaguing the state.

Goal: The ultimate goal is for the user to learn about and agree with the topics on the site, and to persuade the user to donate to the campaign, volunteer for the campaign and vote for the campaign.

Persona

Name: Joe Ortega

Age: 36

Occupation: History teacher at Albuquerque High School

Tech: An apple power user. Uses a Macbook Air M2 chip Mac OS Sonoma 14.2.1, Mac mini M2 chip Mac OS Sonoma 14.2.1, and Iphone 13 IOS 17.3 with unlimited 5G data Connection

Attitudes & Behaviors: Joe is overworked and underpaid. He has to take his work home with him, so he doesn't have much time to browse social media. Joe is in the middle of the road when it comes to his political affiliations. He does like to stay up to date with news and politics, he is somewhat active in the local politics. Joe spends little amount oof time researching

Frustrations & Needs: It is an election year and Joe is actively looking for candidates to vote for in the upcoming election. He knows there are many problems with the state he lives in and has had first-hand experience with those problems, He wants to see the solutions and ideas from the candidates clearly laid out on an easily navigable website. Joe is having a hard time making a final decision, because he knows that politics isn't cut and dry, many politicians are insincere, untrustworthy, and mendacious. Joe wants to make sure he without a doubt he can be cognizant of the issues the candidate is running on and has confidence that they can stay true to their word.

Goals: Joe is mainly concerned with one thing: Do the candidates' priorities and beliefs line up with his, can he give a complete vote of confidence in that person? Joe does not want to spend a long time deciding amongst all the candidates, he wants to easily interact with the candidates site and see intelligible and principled intentions, this will determine if Joe decides to support and vote for the candidate.

User Story: As a user I want to, easily and decisively determine if this candidate aligns with my beliefs and is right for the position of state senator.
