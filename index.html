Nicole's Priorities

Our state is facing critical challenges that demand immediate attention. I’m running for the State Senate because we need solutions that require common sense, innovation and hard work to correct. As your state senator, I will be your advocate and voice. Let’s work together to create a safe and abundant life for ourselves, our families, and future generations.

I am an outsider in this race, bringing a fresh perspective untethered to political establishment. Let’s solve real problems together, right now, and ensure that your concerns are addressed in Santa Fe. Please, join me in creating a brighter future for New Mexico.
